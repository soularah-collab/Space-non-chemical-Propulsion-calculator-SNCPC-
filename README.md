Space-Non-Chemical Space Propulsion Calculator
========================================
The calculator includes:
- Hall-effect thruster estimates
- Gridded ion thruster estimates
- Generic electrospray/FEEP/colloid estimates
- Generic RF plasma, resistojet, and arcjet power-limited estimates
- Pulsed plasma / pulsed inductive thruster estimates
- Magnetoplasmadynamic thruster rough sizing
- Solar sail sizing
- Laser/photon sail sizing
- Electrodynamic tether sizing
- Experimental direct fusion, pulsed fusion, nuclear-pulse-style, and ideal
  antimatter comparison sizing
Important limits
----------------
This is a first-pass engineering calculator. It does not replace plasma
simulation, thermal design, erosion/lifetime testing, power electronics design,
plume contamination analysis, mechanical design, or optimized trajectory tools.
The experimental/fusion tab is especially speculative: it is a parametric
concept calculator, not a validated engine model.

The internet/literature scan was necessarily representative rather than
exhaustive. The calculator uses standard relationships such as thrust = mass
flow x exhaust velocity, Isp = exhaust velocity / g0, power-limited electric
propulsion thrust, electrostatic ion voltage velocity, Tsiolkovsky delta-v,
pulsed impulse-bit thrust, solar radiation pressure, photon momentum, and
electrodynamic tether Lorentz force.

See SOURCES_AND_SCAN_NOTES.md for the research notes and representative papers.
